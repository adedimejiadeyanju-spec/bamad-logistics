Company Overview

Who We Are

Bamad Logistics is a Lagos-based industrial raw materials supplier and trade intermediary, specialising in the procurement and distribution of steel wire rod, cold heading wire (CHQ), and other fastener-grade raw materials across Nigeria and the wider West African sub-region.
Founded to address the persistent supply gap in quality raw materials available to local fastener manufacturers, Bamad Logistics has grown into a trusted procurement partner — connecting international steel mills in Europe, Asia, and the CIS region to Nigeria's fast-growing fastener and metal fabrication industry.
Our model is built on precision sourcing, technical knowledge of steel grades, and a deep understanding of the regulatory and logistics landscape governing importation into West Africa. We do not merely ship metal — we enable industry.
